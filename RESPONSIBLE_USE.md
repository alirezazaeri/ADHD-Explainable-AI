# Responsible use

## Research and technical context

This repository documents a historical machine-learning experiment. It is not a medical device, clinical decision-support system, diagnostic service, or deployment-ready product.

## Prohibited interpretations and uses

Do not use the project to:

- diagnose, screen, treat, or make decisions about a person;
- replace qualified clinical assessment;
- infer causation or biological mechanisms from associations or SHAP values;
- identify biomarkers from Kernel PCA components;
- claim clinical validation, regulatory approval, GDPR compliance, NHS affiliation, or healthcare suitability;
- claim that the model is bias-free, universally fair, or gender-neutral;
- redistribute the dataset or separate participant-level extracts.

## Privacy

The underlying data includes sensitive information about young participants. Keep all separate source, processed, split, and participant-level export files local and protected. Obtain data only from the official provider and comply with its current rules.

Do not add participant rows, identifiers, local explanations, or record-level screenshots to README files, issues, pull requests, discussions, or new documentation.

## Interpreting recorded results

The preserved metrics are affected by known preprocessing and evaluation limitations. They are not estimates of clinical effectiveness or guaranteed performance on unseen populations.

The subgroup metrics are exploratory. Any future high-stakes research would require an independently designed protocol, privacy and ethics review, representative data, external validation, calibrated uncertainty, fairness objectives, domain expertise, and appropriate governance.
